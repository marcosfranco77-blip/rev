I

General Structure of the Book of Revelation

The Three Major Sections of the Tribulation
First Half · Revelation 6–9 · 3½ years 7 Seals, 6 Trumpets, 2 Woe judgments (trumpets 5 and 6). ¼ of the earth dies (seal 4) and ⅓ of men (trumpet 6). The 144,000 are sealed and evangelize the world.
Midpoint of the Tribulation · Revelation 10–15 · A point in time Satan cast out of heaven. Antichrist dies and rises. Abomination of desolation. Mark of the Beast 666. The 2 Witnesses are killed. 144,000 martyrs appear in heaven.
Second Half (Great Tribulation) · Revelation 16–19 · 3½ years Trumpet 7 / Third Woe. 7 Bowl judgments. War of Armageddon. Second Coming of Christ.
After the Tribulation (Chaps. 20–22)
  • ✦
    Chapters 20–22The final defeat of Satan, the Millennium, the New Heaven and the New Earth, and the New Jerusalem.
  • ✦
    Relationship of the JudgmentsThe 7 Seals contain the 7 Trumpets. The 7th Trumpet contains the 7 Bowls. They are nested judgments like Russian nesting dolls, not independent sequences.
  • ✦
    Chronology NoteThe book is not written in strictly chronological order, but in simultaneous scenes. Clues in the text must be sought to identify parallel events.

The Scroll and the Lamb — Revelation Chapter 5

Description of the Scroll
  • ✦
    Written on the inside and outsideThe Israelite method for land title deeds. Written on one side, sealed with wax; when the land was transferred, it was written on the reverse.
  • ✦
    Dual contentOne side contains the promises of the Promised Land; the other, the endorsement of the contract for those lands ceded to the Gentiles.
  • ✦
    The 7 SealsChrist breaks them one by one, unleashing events in heaven and on earth. God has total control over everything that happens.
  • ✦
    The only One worthyNormally opened in the presence of a judge. The only One worthy is the slain Lamb, who reclaims the lands because he paid the price of their redemption.
The Worship of the Lamb (Rev. 5:6–14)
Revelation 5:9 You are worthy to take the scroll, and to open its seals; for You were slain, and have redeemed us to God by Your blood out of every tribe and tongue and people and nation.
  • ✦
    Seven horns and seven eyesThe seven Spirits of God sent out into all the earth.
  • ✦
    Myriads of myriadsThe voice of angels, living creatures, and elders around the throne, acclaiming the Lamb with power, riches, wisdom, strength, honor, glory, and blessing.

2 Thessalonians 2:6–8 And now you know what is restraining… For the mystery of lawlessness is already at work; only He who now restrains will do so until He is taken out of the way. And then the lawless one will be revealed.

  • ✦

2 Thessalonians 2:3–4 …that Day will not come unless the falling away comes first, and the man of sin is revealed, the son of perdition, who opposes and exalts himself above all that is called God or that is worshiped, so that he sits as God in the temple of God, showing himself that he is God.
V

The First Four Seals — The Four Horsemen

Interpretive Principle
  • ✦
    A single riderThe 4 horses are connected under a single rider: the Antichrist progressively raising his power. The horses are symbolic and signify that they ride throughout all the earth.
  • ✦
    Cause and effectEach horse is a consequence of the previous one. This pattern is initiated by Christ breaking each seal — not by the Antichrist. Jesus is the One who drives the advance. (See also Zech. 6:1–8)
  • ✦
    Historical analogyThis pattern was seen with Hitler, but the Antichrist is of greater magnitude and is the last.
First Seal — White Horse White
Revelation 6:1–2 And I looked, and behold, a white horse. He who sat on it had a bow; and a crown was given to him, and he went out conquering and to conquer.
  • ✦
    Bow without arrowsThreat of war without actual war. Like a gun without bullets: intimidation and coercion.
  • ✦
    Crown (stephanos)An earned crown, not inherited. A leader who obtained power by his own means, possibly with some military prowess. He achieves the Middle East peace treaty, which gives him global power and recognition.
Second Seal — Red Horse Red
Revelation 6:3–4 Another horse, fiery red, went out. And it was granted to the one who sat on it to take peace from the earth, and that people should kill one another; and there was given to him a great sword.
  • ✦
    SwordAn actual weapon of war, very different from the bow without arrows. Now real war comes.
  • ✦
    Red — bloodSymbolizes war and bloodshed. The Antichrist's power reaches the point of taking peace from the earth. World wars, with modern technology: nuclear, AI, cognitive warfare.
  • ✦
    Cognitive Warfare"Includes activities to affect attitudes and behaviors by influencing, protecting, or disrupting cognition at the individual, group, or population level." (NATO)
Third Seal — Black Horse Black
Revelation 6:5–6 So I looked, and behold, a black horse, and he who sat on it had a pair of scales in his hand. And I heard a voice in the midst of the four living creatures saying, "A quart of wheat for a denarius, and three quarts of barley for a denarius; and do not harm the oil and the wine."
  • ✦
    ScalesSymbolizes market prices. Extreme hyperinflation: a loaf of bread could cost $60, provoking riots, theft, and famines.
  • ✦
    BlackDeath as a result of war and famine.
  • ✦
    Oil and winePossibly representing Israel (olives and grapes): hyperinflation and war will not touch Israel up to a point. The world is being shaped to ultimately unify against Israel.
  • ✦
    NoteThe cost of goods is determined from the center of God's throne.
Fourth Seal — Pale Horse Pale
Revelation 6:7–8 So I looked, and behold, a pale horse. And the name of him who sat on it was Death, and Hades followed with him. And power was given to them over a fourth of the earth, to kill with sword, with hunger, with death, and by the beasts of the earth.
  • ✦
    Color "Chloros"Yellowish-green in Greek: the color of a body days after death.
  • ✦
    A fourth of the earth22 million dead in WWI; 66 million in WWII; 2 billion projected in WWIII. They have increased in severity like birth pains.
  • ✦
    HadesHell follows the death of the unbeliever. The final result is: death followed by hell.

Fifth Seal — Saints and Martyrs of the Tribulation

The Martyrs Under the Altar
Revelation 6:9–11 I saw under the altar the souls of those who had been slain for the word of God and for the testimony which they held… And it was said to them that they should rest a little while longer, until both the number of their fellow servants and their brethren, who would be killed as they were, was completed.
  • ✦
    Who they areDisembodied spirits — souls who believed and died after the Rapture. They did not receive a body in the resurrection of the Church, so they died during the Tribulation.
  • ✦
    They are not the Church saintsThe Church has already been taken to heaven with a body. These are the first martyrs of Revelation, but not the last.
Characteristics of Seal 5
  • ✦
    Breaks the patternThere is no "come and see." It takes place in heaven, not on earth. It represents the full 7 years, not a single moment.
  • ✦
    The believer's way of lifeBeing a Christian during the Tribulation is extremely dangerous. The vast majority will be martyrs. Death for the believer will be better than remaining alive through the Tribulation.
  • ✦
    Key contrastSeal 4: Hades follows the death of the unbeliever. Seal 5: the Throne of Heaven follows the death of the believer. Seal 5 balances seal 4.
  • ✦
    CausalityDirect cause: Jesus opening the seals. Indirect cause: the Antichrist.
Daniel on the Tribulation Saints
Daniel 7:25–27 He shall speak pompous words against the Most High, shall persecute the saints of the Most High, and shall intend to change times and law. Then the saints shall be given into his hand for a time and times and half a time. But the court shall be seated, and they shall take away his dominion, to consume and destroy it forever.
VII

Sixth Seal — Supernatural Calamities

The Phenomena of Seal 6
Revelation 6:12–14 And behold, there was a great earthquake; and the sun became black as sackcloth of hair, and the moon became like blood. And the stars of heaven fell to the earth… Then the sky receded as a scroll when it is rolled up, and every mountain and island was moved out of its place.
  • ✦
    Unambiguously supernaturalThe first 5 seals could be attributed to human actions. This seal rules them out completely. The laws of physics will be broken.
  • ✦
    Physical consequencesIf the sun does not shine: below-zero temperatures, hurricanes, earthquakes, volcanoes. People would seek warmth underground.
  • ✦
    Stars fallingProbably a meteor shower (not angels, given the context of celestial bodies).
Humanity's Reaction (Rev. 6:15–17)
Revelation 6:16–17 And said to the mountains and rocks, "Fall on us and hide us from the face of Him who sits on the throne and from the wrath of the Lamb! For the great day of His wrath has come, and who is able to stand?"
  • ✦
    Belated recognitionAt this point, people finally recognize that the events come directly from the throne of God. Yet they do not repent — they complain against Him.
  • ✦
    Who evangelized them?If the Church is no longer on earth, who opened their knowledge of God and Jesus? Chapter 7 answers it partially; chapter 11 explains it fully.

The 144,000 Sealed — Chapter 7

The Sealing — Narrative Context
  • ✦
    Not chronologicalChapter 7 is a parenthesis describing events that happened in the background during the first seals. Rev. 7:1–2 describes simultaneous events.
  • ✦
    The 4 angelsThey hold back the 4 winds of the earth (judgment of devastation: without wind there is no rain, water stagnates, famines), allowing some to be sealed first.
  • ✦
    The sealThe Holy Spirit marking the saved. On their foreheads, indicating they will not receive the Mark of the Beast. (2 Cor. 1:21–22)
The 144,000 — Who They Are
  • ✦
    All are Jews12,000 from each tribe of Israel. The salvations are evidence that the Holy Spirit returned to the earth. None were believers before the Rapture.
  • ✦
    Dan excludedJoseph replaces Dan (divided into Manasseh and Ephraim). Dan was excluded possibly for introducing idolatry into Israel. This does not mean those from Dan will not be saved.
  • ✦
    Virgin menAccording to Rev. 14, they were all virgin males.
  • ✦
    Without human evangelismNo one evangelized them. Like Paul's conversion, God is the author of their faith. It demonstrates that salvation is a sovereign work of God.
Significance of the Number 12 × 12 = 144,000
  • ✦
    The number 12 in the BibleRepresents God's government through human leaders. It is the beginning of God's new house on earth. Similar to the 12 apostles chosen by Jesus.
  • ✦
    The Jewish tradition of salvationThe patriarchs, prophets, apostles, and Jesus are all Jewish. The Old and New Covenant, the Word, the temple service: everything related to salvation has come to humanity through the Jewish people.
  • ✦
    Evangelistic seedThe 144,000 lead a worldwide evangelistic wave during the Tribulation. This verse represents evangelism throughout the entire Tribulation.

The Seventh Seal — Prelude to the 7 Trumpets

The Silence and the Censer (Rev. 8:1–5)
Revelation 8:1 When He opened the seventh seal, there was silence in heaven for about half an hour.
  • ✦
    30 minutes of silenceProfound intensity. The longer the silence, the more painful the series of judgments. It predicts the magnitude of the birth pains that will precede the birth of the new age with Christ's coming.
  • ✦
    The prayers of the saintsAn angel adds incense to the prayers of all the saints on the altar. There are believers on earth asking for protection, defense, and relief.
  • ✦
    Fire cast downThe angel fills the censer with fire from the altar and casts it to the earth: thunderings, voices, lightnings, and an earthquake — prelude to the trumpets.
Structure of the Nested Judgments
  • ❶
    7 SealsThe 7th seal contains the 7 trumpets.
  • ❷
    7 TrumpetsThe first 6 trumpets occur in the first half of the Tribulation. The 7th trumpet contains the 7 bowls.
  • ❸
    7 BowlsThey correspond to the Great Tribulation (second half). They are the most intense judgments.
  • ✦
    The trumpetsThey are a warning sign. The Lord warns the earth that the end is near. First 4: physical devastation of the earth. Last 3: terrible impact on people ("Woe judgments").
Temporal location of the seals and trumpets The seals and the first 6 trumpets occur during the first half of the Tribulation — before the Antichrist is possessed by Satan and sits in the Temple stopping the sacrifices.

First Trumpet — Hail and Fire
Revelation 8:7 And hail and fire followed, mingled with blood, and they were thrown to the earth. And a third of the trees were burned up, and all green grass was burned up.
  • ✦
    Parallel with EgyptSimilar to the judgment of hail and fire in Exodus 9:23–24, but on a greater scale.
  • ✦
    A contiguous thirdThe destruction probably affects a geographically contiguous third of the earth, not evenly distributed. This pattern continues in the next trumpet.
Second Trumpet — The Sea Turned to Blood
Revelation 8:8–9 Something like a great mountain burning with fire was thrown into the sea, and a third of the sea became blood. And a third of the living creatures in the sea died, and a third of the ships were destroyed.
  • ✦
    Great mountainCould be a meteor, volcano, or supernatural event. None of them naturally turn water to blood.
  • ✦
    Why contiguousConcentrated blood kills fish and sinks ships (alters buoyancy). Diluted throughout all the water would not produce those specific effects.
  • ✦
    Geopolitical conclusionThe Lord narrows the focus toward the final stage: the region between Jerusalem and Babylon. At the end of the 7 years, only that area will be the zone of the final battle.
Third Trumpet — Wormwood
Revelation 8:10–11 A great star fell from heaven, burning like a torch, and it fell on a third of the rivers and on the springs of water. The name of the star is Wormwood… and many men died from the water, because it was made bitter.
  • ✦
    Star = Fallen angelThe names of God's angels end in "EL" (Michael, Gabriel, etc.). "Wormwood" does not. It could be a demonic angel assigned for this purpose.
  • ✦
    Planetary supernatural eventThe third of rivers and springs is not in a single geographical location, hence it requires a supernatural action that affects the entire planet but only in a third of it.
Fourth Trumpet — Cosmic Darkness
Revelation 8:12 Then a third of the sun was struck, a third of the moon, and a third of the stars, so that a third of them were darkened. A third of the day did not shine, and likewise the night.
  • ✦
    Astronomically accurateWhen the sun does not shine on one side of the earth, the moon cannot reflect light to the other side either. This is consistent with the solar system, though the Bible was written before this was known.
  • ✦
    Consequences of ⅓ less energyDramatic drop in temperature, frozen waters, death of livestock and crops, more deaths from cold — added to those already caused by previous judgments.
Situation Summary at This Point (Trumpet 4)
  • ✦
    ¼ of the population deadFrom famines, pestilences, and war (seal 4).
  • ✦
    ⅓ of grass and trees burnedTrumpet 1.
  • ✦
    ⅓ of the sea turned to blood⅓ of ships and marine living creatures dead. Trumpet 2.
  • ✦
    ⅓ of rivers and springs contaminatedTrumpet 3.
  • ✦
    The earth: uninhabitableSurvivors at this point have endured extremely difficult conditions. Life on earth resembles a miniature hell — which produces a more receptive audience to the gospel. Meanwhile, the 144,000 continue preaching.
Where Are the United States and the Americas?
Geographic hypothesis (author's assumption) The American continent corresponds to approximately "one third" of the total land and is diametrically opposite Israel. The Pacific Ocean represents "one third" of the remaining seas, opposite the Middle East. This suggests that the destruction could begin on this side and progress toward the final stage in the Middle East. Note: In the end, it does not matter where it begins — the final focus will always be the Middle East.

Fifth Trumpet (First Woe) — The Locusts from the Abyss

The Bottomless Pit — Biblical Cosmology
  • ✦
    The fallen starA fallen angel (possibly Satan or Abaddon/Apollyon — "the destroyer") receives the key to the bottomless pit and opens it.
  • ✦
    Sheol and its sectionsIt has two parts separated by an impassable chasm (Luke 16:26): Abraham's Bosom (a pleasant place — now empty since Christ's resurrection) and the Sheol of torment. There is also a specific prison for disobedient angels.
  • ✦
    The imprisoned angelsExtremely disobedient angels from the time of Noah — they had relations with human women, fathering giants who corrupted the human race. As a consequence, they were imprisoned. (2 Pet. 2:4)
  • ✦
    The smoke of the pitSmoke comes out that darkens the sun and the air — confirming that hell is beneath the earth. The lava and smoke of volcanoes possibly have a connection. Science does not understand the depths of the earth beyond 2–3 miles.
The 5-Month Torment (Rev. 9:3–11)
Revelation 9:5–6 And they were not given authority to kill them, but to torment them for five months… In those days men will seek death and will not find it; they will desire to die, and death will flee from them.
  • ✦
    Specific instructionsDo not harm the vegetation. Only the men without the seal of God on their foreheads. The 144,000 and believers are protected. (2 Pet. 2:9)
  • ✦
    Scorpion-like tormentScorpion venom is so toxic it is used in chemotherapy. This lasts 5 months — a devastating psychological effect. Suicide will not be possible.
  • ✦
    The number 5 = GraceGod allows them to experience hell temporarily rather than dying and going to eternal hell. It is a form of grace: a foretaste of what hell will be, while there is still time to avoid it.
  • ✦
    A unique moment in historyFrom the Garden of Eden until now, this will be the only moment when physical death is impossible.

  • ✦
    Prepared for the exact momentSo terrible and depraved that if they had not been bound they would have killed people all along. God prepared them beforehand for this specific hour.
  • ✦

The Demonic Horses (Rev. 9:17–19)

Revelation 9:20–21 But the rest of mankind, who were not killed by these plagues, did not repent of the works of their hands, that they should not worship demons… And they did not repent of their murders or their sorceries or their sexual immorality or their thefts.

✦

The first half of the Tribulation reveals a fundamental truth: God has absolute control over everything that occurs in heaven and on earth. It is Jesus who breaks each seal and unleashes each judgment. The Antichrist is only an instrument, not the author of the events. Despite unprecedented devastation — wars, famines, plagues, cosmic darkness, and demonic armies — God's grace continues to work through the 144,000 sealed Jews, producing an innumerable harvest of souls from all nations. Calamity never saves: it is solely God's grace, working through faith, that transforms the human heart.
